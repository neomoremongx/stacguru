Our Story

Opened in 2013, Food Studio strives to offer a wide variety of products to serve every customer's need. From homemade pies, sweet treats, ciabatta sandwiches, hot drinks and cold beverages. You name it, and we've got it!

Food Studio also offers the opportunity to host and cater for small functions. All of this, in the heart of Potchefstroom, is offered in a unique environment and atmosphere you've never experienced before.
