About Corne Design

Corne Design Architecture is a premier architectural firm based in Potchefstroom, South Africa. With a passion for innovative design and meticulous attention to detail, we transform architectural visions into reality.

Our approach combines creative excellence with practical functionality, ensuring that every project we undertake not only meets but exceeds our clients' expectations. From initial concept to final construction, we guide you through every step of the architectural process.
