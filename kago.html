Our Story

About Kago Entle

Kago Entle is a premier catering and events company based in Klerksdorp, Northwest province. With years of experience in the industry, we pride ourselves on delivering exceptional service and creating memorable experiences for our clients.

We offer comprehensive event solutions, from furniture and décor rentals to full event coordination for weddings, corporate functions, kiddies parties, seminars, and more. Our team works closely with you to bring your vision to life from start to finish at affordable rates.
