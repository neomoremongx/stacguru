About PJ Carpentry

With years of experience in the woodworking industry, PJ Carpentry has established itself as a trusted name for quality craftsmanship in Potchefstroom and surrounding areas.

We take pride in transforming raw materials into beautiful, functional pieces that stand the test of time. Whether it's custom cabinetry, outdoor furniture, or restoring a family heirloom, we approach every project with precision and passion.
